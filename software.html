  • MAVProxy:
    • Version: 1.8.48
    • At the field our TX2 IP was 192.168.1.149 and the laptop with GCS was 192.168.1.139.
    • We had one application connect to MAVProxy through port 14551. The GCS used port 14550.
    • From the TX2 terminal we ran:
    • mavproxy.py --master=/dev/ttyACM0 --out=udp:192.168.1.139:14550 --out=udp:127.0.0.1:14551
